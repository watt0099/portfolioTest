ABOUT

Nice to meet you!

I'm Alex Watt, a digital designer. I have a background in web design and development. Recently I obtained a post-graduate certificate in Interactive Media Management with Honours. From that program, I was able to get a deeper understanding of the best modern web practices, as well as valuable training in other forms of media.

In my spare time I can be found working on my art, wandering the woods, or playing with my little girl.

Living in Ottawa

Avaliable to work in town, and remotely.
